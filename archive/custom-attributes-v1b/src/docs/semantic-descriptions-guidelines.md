# Semantic descriptions (M0)

Purpose:

- Help humans understand what a field captures and when it matters.
- Provide structured hints for future AI agents (M2) that read/write data.

Write 1–3 sentences covering:

1. What this field captures
2. When/why it's used
3. Example values (if useful)

Keep language clear and concrete. Avoid internal jargon.

Examples:

- **Risk category**: "Primary classification for reporting and ownership. Choose the option that best represents the risk's nature (e.g., Operational, Strategic)."
- **Risk owner**: "Accountable individual for this risk. Must be someone who can approve mitigation actions."
- **Inherent risk score**: "Baseline risk level before controls are applied. Used to compare risks across the portfolio."
